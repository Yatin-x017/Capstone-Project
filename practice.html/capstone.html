FAQ's — quick & messy

Below are some quick answers. This is intentionally a bit rough — like a page someone slapped together.

How do I buy something?
Click the "Add to Cart" button on a product. Then go to the Cart and follow checkout steps. Payment methods: card, maybe other stuff later.
How do I sell on HandmadeMarket?
Hit "Sell" in the header. You'll be prompted to create a seller profile. It's basic right now — upload pics, write a description, set price, publish. We may add fees later.
Can I return an item?
Depends on the seller. Some accept returns, some don't. Check each listing or contact seller directly. We try to encourage clear return policies.
Where are items shipped from?
Sellers are independent and ship from various locations. Check listing for estimated shipping times and locations.
Is my payment secure?
Basic protections are in place, but this is a minimal demo. Do not use real payment info in this unrefined build.
